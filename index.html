Hi, my name is Josh Hansan. I work in the healthcare investments / diligence space.

My work includes deep dives into the markets across the entire healthcare sector, but my main interests are pre-clinical assets (led by solid tumors + neurogenetic diseases), consumer health tech, preventative medicine / longevity, payor mechanisms, and provider-centric behavior.

In my spare time, I focus my readings and writings on domestic policy programs, American history, futurism, financial technology, general health and wellness (i.e., poorly extrapolating the results of small single-arm observational trials to my life), and college athletics.
